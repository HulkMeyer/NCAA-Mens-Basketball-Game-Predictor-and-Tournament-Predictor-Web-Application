March Madness Game Simulator

Welcome to the March Madness Bracket Simulator!

Set the Simualation Controls to your liking and the system will simulate all games from from the First Four to the Championship. Good Luck!

Simulation Controls Explained

Chaos Level (Noise Std): Controls randomness in score predictions.
• Low (0.5): Very stable outcomes, few upsets.
• Medium (2.5): Balanced realism and unpredictability.
• High (5.0): Wild swings, frequent upsets and score volatility.

Seed Consistency Bias: Adjusts how much top seeds resist randomness.
• Low (0.5): Top seeds dominate, fewer surprises.
• Medium (0.8): Realistic balance of consistency and chaos.
• High (1.2): All seeds treated more equally, more parity.

Model vs History: Blends predictive model with historical seed win rates.
• Low (0.0): Pure model logic, no historical bias.
• Medium (0.5): Balanced between model and history.
• High (1.0): Pure historical outcomes, less model influence.

Normalize Scores by Round: Aligns scores with historical round averages.
• Yes: Scores reflect typical round scoring (e.g. lower in Final Four).
• No: Raw model outputs used, may be inflated or deflated.
